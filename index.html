About Us

Scrap Daddies are a Mumbai-based startup on a mission to foster a greener India. We connect businesses and individuals with scrap recyclers to ensure responsible and eco-friendly disposal of waste. Join us in building a sustainable future for our nation! 🌱♻️

Our aim is to create a widespread and impactful recycling initiative in India by collecting scrap material from every individual household, company, shop, and various other sectors. We are dedicated to promoting proper recycling practices to protect the environment, conserve natural resources, and reduce waste pollution.
